When you said, "The cake was mouthwatering. Many thanks," it made me happier than I expected. Even knowing it wasn't perfect, even hearing it was a little salty, it didn't matter.

Because it came from you.

When you sent "chi muuhai" " muu hun" I couldn't help but laugh.

By the last game, I just wanted to stay in that moment a little longer. When you asked for a trade and I accepted, and you said "thanks," that small word stayed with me longer than it should have.

The next day, on the twentieth, you accepted my follow request. At last, I could see your pictures. I even posted a "happy man" story on Instagram. As I looked through your photos, I found myself smiling shyly and unexpectedly happy, even though you weren't anywhere near me.

Your smile had a way of staying, even when everything else was quiet...
