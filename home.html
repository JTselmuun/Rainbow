A story about wonder, weather, and becoming

Welcome

When J was little, he loved rainbows.

He liked how they only appeared when the sun and the rain met, like something special had to happen first.

Every time he saw one, he would start running toward it. He knew he couldn't reach it, but that didn't stop him.

He just kept going, running and running until his legs grew tired and his breath ran out.

And even then, he didn't feel sad, because for a little while, he was chasing something beautiful.
