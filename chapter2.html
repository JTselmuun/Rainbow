Chapter 1: Red - Miracle

On a bright, windy day, my phone buzzed. A friend asked me if I could work as a part-time translator in mid-March. For the past two years, I had always turned down that offer. Around that time every year, Around that time every year, I usually went to Wakayama, where I lived for three years, so I wasn't sure whether to go there like always or take the job instead.

The wind was strong that day, strong enough that I had to raise my voice just to speak. It felt strange, almost like something was pushing me forward. I didn't really understand why, but this year felt different.

So I chose differently. I didn't go to Wakayama. Instead, I accepted the four-day translator job at FOODEX. I wasn't completely sure about my decision, but I told myself, what's done is done.

The first two days passed like any other event. It was busy, loud, and nothing really stood out. During my breaks, I walked around and tried foods from different countries, just passing time without thinking too much.

But on the third day, something small changed.

I didn't take my break early.
