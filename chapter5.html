Chapter 4: Green - Growth

The rainy days kept continuing, and somehow, I kept thinking of you more than before. It wasn't something I tried to do, it just happened naturally. Even when I was doing something else, your name would come into my mind without any reason, and I would stay there for a while, just thinking.

Just thinking about you made me want to become a better person. Not in a big or dramatic way, but in small, quiet ways that only I could notice. I started doing more things, trying new hobbies, and giving more effort to things I used to ignore.

Maybe it's simple. When you like someone, you start thinking about the kind of person you should be, and the kind of person they might like someday. Even if nothing changes between you, even if they don't notice, you still want to move forward in your own way.

I knew things weren't going the way I had imagined, and I knew you might not feel the same way. But even then, it didn't feel like a bad thing.

Having someone you like isn't a bad thing, even if you're being ignored.
