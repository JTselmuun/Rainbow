On the fourth day, in my dream, I was lying on my bed when I got a message from you. I couldn't believe it. You wrote, "Let's meet." I replied, "Tonight? Tomorrow? I'm available anytime." And then you said, "Now." I was so happy, and I was about to get ready, but I woke up.

The cruel thing about dreams is how real they feel until they end.

I wanted to meet you before your cousin went far away, but I couldn't, and now it feels like the chances of meeting you have become smaller than before.

Still, if you ever have time to grab lunch or something, just tell me. I hope you eat well and sleep well, and if you ever need someone to talk to or someone's help, don't hesitate to ask.
