Chapter 2: Orange - Spark

After that day, I couldn't stop thinking about you.

It wasn't anything big or dramatic. You were just there, quietly staying in my mind. The way you spoke, the way you smiled, even that small moment when you said, "Teach me how" kept coming back again and again.

I found myself checking my phone more than usual, even though I knew nothing new had happened yet. Still, I wanted to see you again, so I texted you.

I asked if you would still be around in the afternoon. When you replied "yes," I felt something simple but strong relief, maybe even excitement. It wasn't a long message, but it was enough to change my whole mood.

I asked what kind of chocolate you liked, and you said anything except dark chocolate. Then I went to find you.

We met outside Tokyo Big Sight.

This time, it wasn't by chance anymore.
