Chapter 5: Blue - Mystery

I know a little about you, but not enough. You are still a mystery I want to understand. I know that you are beautiful, about 163 cm tall, and I know your name, your school, and your major. I know you are a good cook, and that you are good at drawing and painting. But everything else still feels unknown.

Sometimes, when I eat something delicious, I think of you and wish you could try it too. When I go somewhere nice, or see a field full of flowers, I think of you again and wish you could see it. Even in those small moments, you are there, quietly staying in my thoughts.

Even in my dreams, you are still a mystery. For four days straight, you appeared in my dreams. I don't remember the first two clearly, but on the third night, you were wearing a black blazer and slightly loose light blue jeans, just like in your Instagram picture. You kept smiling, and your smile melted me.

Somehow, in that dream, I told you, "I will marry you." You smiled and were just about to say something, but morning stole the words before I could hear them.
